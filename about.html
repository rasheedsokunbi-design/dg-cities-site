About DG Cities

Data and insights with impact — people first

Established by the Royal Borough of Greenwich in 2015, we specialise in data-driven asset management, housing innovation, retrofit and decarbonisation, IoT deployment, and impact evaluation.

Our mission

Who we are

DG Cities works at the intersection of housing operations, asset strategy, compliance, technology providers and data integration platforms — helping landlords move from reactive approaches to intelligence-led decision making.

We combine quantitative and qualitative approaches to create a rich understanding of any project or approach, and work with clients to explore what works, what doesn't, and ways to improve.

We have experience working with the Royal Borough of Greenwich, Hackney Council, the Department for Energy Security and Net Zero, OFGEM and UKRI.

Since 2015

Advancing innovation in cities — practical approaches that deliver real results for residents and housing providers

Our work spans housing operations, asset strategy, compliance, technology and data integration.
